Space Conquest

About the Game

Welcome to Space Conquest, an immersive 4X (eXplore, eXpand, eXploit, and eXterminate) turn-based strategy game set in the vast expanse of space. Command your civilization as you:

  • Explore mysterious star systems and discover valuable resources
  • Expand your empire by colonizing new worlds
  • Exploit resources and develop advanced technologies
  • Engage in epic space battles with rival civilizations
